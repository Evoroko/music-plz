MercuryKX

What is MercuryKX ?

Originally coming from Universal Music UK/Decca Recoards, MercuryKX is a branch oriented towards post-classical pop launched in 2017. It is managed by Cerys Weetch and Hildur Maral, and focuses on the edges of electronic, instrumental and vocal experimentation with artists from all around the world.

They’re not only a label of musicians — graphic designers, poets, and photographers are working together to achieve a unique identity. Their productions are often bathed in landscapes and sightseeing, with compositions laying in serenity, passing through turbulent waters towards calming shores.

Main artists

  • Ólafur Arnalds

    Icelandic musician

    Ólafur Arnalds is an Icelandic composer and musician. He plays guitar, drums, and invented the multi-layered Stratus Piano.He published 14 albums and has made a 140+ date world tour in 2018–2019.

  • Anoushka Shankar

    Woman icon

    Anoushka Shankar is a British-American sitar player, producer, film composer and activist. She was the youngest and first woman to receive a British House of Commons Shield; she has had 7 Grammy Awards nominations and was the first musician of Indian origin to perform live and to serve as presenter at the ceremony.[1] She performs across multiple genres and styles - classical and contemporary, acoustic and electronic.

  • Jean-Michel Blais

    Technical and classical

    Jean-Michel Blais is a composer and post-classical pianist from Montreal, Canada. He weaves beauty and technical prowess in stunning piano pieces.

  • Isobel Waller-Bridge

    The variety of art

    Isobel Noeline Waller-Bridge is a British composer who is known for her scores for film, television, and theatre, along with her works for electronic music and contemporary classical music.
